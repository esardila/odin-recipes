Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious homemade lasagna recipe with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheeses. A classic comfort food that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, and salt. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese and egg together.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat layers.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 25 minutes.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
