Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chunks of grilled marinated chicken simmered in a rich, creamy spiced tomato sauce. A beloved Indian-inspired dish full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, turmeric, and 1 tsp garam masala. Marinate chicken for 1 hour.
  2. Grill or broil chicken until lightly charred. Set aside.
  3. In a large pan, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and remaining garam masala. Simmer 15 minutes.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  6. Add cooked chicken and cook 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve over basmati rice or with naan bread.
