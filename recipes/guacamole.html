Guacamole

Description

Fresh and creamy avocado dip with lime, cilantro, and a kick of jalapeño. Perfect as a snack or party appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
  • 1 Roma tomato, diced
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Halve avocados, remove pits, and scoop flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash avocados to desired consistency with a fork.
  3. Stir in lime juice, cilantro, onion, jalapeño, and tomato.
  4. Season with salt and mix well.
  5. Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
