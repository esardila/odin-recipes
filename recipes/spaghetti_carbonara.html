Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Rich, creamy, and ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 8 oz pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a skillet, cook pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, Parmesan, and black pepper together.
  4. Remove skillet from heat. Add hot spaghetti and toss.
  5. Pour egg mixture over pasta, tossing quickly to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water as needed.
  6. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan on top.
